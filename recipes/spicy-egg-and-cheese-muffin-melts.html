Spicy Egg and Cheese Muffin Melts

These spicy egg and cheese muffin melts stack jalapenos, green onion, a fried egg, and Cheddar slices on top of toasted English muffin halves. A turn under the broiler makes these delicious for wither breakfast or lunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 English muffin, split
  • 1 tablespoon salted butter, divided
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 heaping teaspoons minced jalapeno or other chile pepper
  • 1 green onion, divided
  • 4 thin slices sharp Cheddar cheese
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Line a small baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Toast English muffin in a toaster on the bagel setting until crispy. Spread muffin with about 1 tablespoon butter, then lay butter side up on the prepared pan.
  3. Melt remaining butter in a skillet over medium-low heat. Crack eggs into the skillet and season with salt and pepper. Fry eggs to taste, turning once if desired.
  4. Mix jalapeno and a spoonful of green onion together in a small bowl;. Scatter mixture over both muffin halves.
  5. Transfer 1 egg on top of each muffin half. Lay 2 slices cheese onto each egg.
  6. Turn on the ovenâ€™s broiler. Broil in the oven until cheese is bubbling. Serve with remaining green onion as a garnish.
